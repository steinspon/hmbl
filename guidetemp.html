Sauna Shutoff Guide

Use this guide to confirm that the sauna is fully shut down before leaving the cabin.

Step 1

Locate the sauna control dials and confirm you are looking at the correct heater controls.

Step 2

Turn the left dial fully back to zero. Confirm that the setting is not left on a timer or delayed start position.

Step 3

Check that the sauna is no longer heating and that the heater is cooling down safely before you leave.
